Recipe Instructions

  1. Marinade chicken in sweet white wine for 2 hours
  2. Butterfly chicken breasts and pound to even thickness
  3. Saute in olive oil until golden brown (3-4 mins per side)
  4. Add sliced mushrooms and cook for 2 minutes
  5. Drizzle with white wine and reduce by half
  6. Serve with steamed vegetables
