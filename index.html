Spaghetti Bolognese Recipe
Ingredients:
  • Spaghetti
  • Ground beef
  • Tomato sauce
  • Garlic
  • Parmesan cheese
Directions:
  1. Cook spaghetti according to package instructions.
  2. Brown ground beef and garlic in a pan.
  3. Add tomato sauce and simmer for 20 minutes.
  4. Serve the sauce over spaghetti and top with Parmesan cheese.
